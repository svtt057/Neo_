NEO - User Guide

Getting Started

  1. Download and install the NEO app.
  2. Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.
  3. Connect your calendar, email, and project management tools.

Using NEO

NEO will automatically organize your tasks based on priority and deadlines. You can also add tasks manually and set reminders.

To use NEO's natural language processing capabilities, simply type your requests or questions into the chat interface.

Additional Features

  • Goal setting and tracking
  • Time management tools
  • Productivity analytics
